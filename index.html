Work Experience

War Robot Hardware Engineer
September 2016 - December 2016

When I was studing at lyceum one of my friend offered to organize a competition of “RobotWar” like in Russian TV show. And then with our team members, we started the process with our team and with the help of lyceum facilitators.

Firstly, we made outer shell like tank form. And then in the inner part we used Arduino, h-bridge, accumulator, Bluetooth and motor in order to propel the robot.

While Arduino is the brain of our robot, accumulator and h-bridge helps to control concentration and Bluetooth is used to control the robot with help of cellphone.

Finally, we finished the work with making the outer part of the robot like tank. First time we got the 3rd place in the competition, and the following year we eventually got the 1st place.
